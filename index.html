Welcome to the LFC Irish Supporters Website!

Welcome to the new website specifically for Irish supporters of Liverpool F.C.

Liverpool Football Club is an English Premier League football club based in Liverpool, England. Liverpool F.C. is one of the most successful clubs in England and has won more European trophies than any other English team with five European Cups, three UEFA Cups and three UEFA Super Cups. The club has also won eighteen League titles, seven FA Cups and a record eight League Cups. Liverpool was founded in 1892 and joined the Football League the following year. The club has played at Anfield since its formation.
